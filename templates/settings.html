Email Templates

Each connected calendar has its own Prep and Handoff email templates, since every calendar represents a separate client-facing brand. Pick a calendar below to view or edit its templates.

Prep Email Template

This intro text is prepended to the Client-Facing Notes when sending the "Send Prep Email" from a deal card. Always sent from Runway Selling regardless of calendar. Only available once Step 3 (add the team to the meeting invite) is complete on a deal. Can still be edited per-email before sending.

Handoff Email Template

Sent to the customer (from the original booking) from whichever calendar mailbox the deal's meeting was actually booked on — matching the calendar picked above — CC'ing the Account Manager and Sales Executive, to introduce their team. Only available once Step 3 is complete on a deal. This email does not include the client-facing notes — template only. Use [[first_name]], [[account_manager]], and [[sales_executive]] as placeholders — they're replaced with the customer's first name and the assigned names before sending. Editable per-email before sending.
